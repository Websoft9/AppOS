Resources Homepage Design Directions

These six directions explore different balances of clarity, speed, grouping strength, and professional weight for the AppOS Resources homepage. All of them keep the same core model: a blue-led, restrained, click-first resource entry surface.

The balanced default. Calm grouping, moderate card weight, clear path to Add Resource.
